## Lunge Matrix
### Muscles
  - quadriceps
  - hamstring
  - gluteus maximus
  - gluteus medial
### Description
Useful to do an airplane first, 5 reps each side
The matrix is 5 types of lunges, 10 reps of each
#### Sagital plane - forward lunge, arms and knees
#### Lunge with twist
#### Side lunge 
(HIGH, not cossack)
#### Back and to the side - 45 degrees
#### Backwards lunge
